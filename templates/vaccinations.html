Pet Vaccination Schedule

Age (Weeks) Vaccine Notes
6-8 DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza) Core vaccine; first dose. Often combined.
6-8 Bordetella (Kennel Cough) Non-core; recommended for social/boarding dogs.
10-12 DHPP (2nd dose) Booster shot.
12-16 Rabies Core; legally required in most regions.
12-16 Leptospirosis (1st dose) Non-core; for dogs in wet/high-risk areas.
14-16 DHPP (3rd dose) Final puppy shot.
14-16 Leptospirosis (2nd dose) Booster if needed.
16+ Lyme Disease (1st dose) Non-core; for tick-prone areas.
1 year later All boosters (DHPP, Rabies, etc.) Follow-up boosters (frequency varies by vaccine).

üê± Cats

Age (Weeks) Vaccine Notes
6-8 FVRCP (Panleukopenia, Herpesvirus, Calicivirus) Core; first dose.
8-10 FeLV (Feline Leukemia, 1st dose) Non-core; for outdoor/multi-cat households.
10-12 FVRCP (2nd dose) Booster shot.
12-14 FeLV (2nd dose) Final kitten dose if at risk.
12-16 Rabies Core; legally required in many regions.
14-16 FVRCP (3rd dose) Final core kitten vaccine.
1 year later All boosters (FVRCP, Rabies, FeLV) Follow-up boosters (frequency varies).

General Notes:

  • Core Vaccines (green): Essential for all pets
  • Non-Core Vaccines (orange): Given based on lifestyle/risk
  • Puppies/kittens need vaccines every 3-4 weeks until 16 weeks for full protection
